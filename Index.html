Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 17, 2026

Overview

Inserable ("we", "our", or "the app") is an image management and Markdown editing tool designed for Hugo static blogs, developed by Zixiang Zhang. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

Inserable processes all image data and Markdown content locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit your files or content to any external servers.

Information We Collect

Inserable is designed with privacy in mind. Here is what we collect:

Information We DO NOT Collect:

  • Image files or visual content
  • Markdown article content
  • File paths or directory structures of your blog
  • Personal identification information
  • Location data
  • Contact information

Information Stored Locally:

  • App preferences (such as Image Root and Markdown Root folder paths) - stored only on your device to enable functionality

How We Process Your Data

When you use Inserable to standardize images, bind Markdown files, or edit content:

  • All file operations and renaming happen entirely on your local file system
  • No data is sent to external servers or cloud services
  • We cannot access your images or blog posts at any time

Third-Party Services

Inserable does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. It is a local utility tool.

Data Security

Since all data processing occurs locally on your device and we do not collect personal information, your data security is maintained by your device's built-in security features and your own file management practices.

Children's Privacy

Inserable does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. The app is a technical utility tool that does not require any personal information to function.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
